The Full-Stop She Never Put

Last day of college — everyone was busy celebrating the send-off. Meanwhile, I was on the terrace, thinking I’m about to lose my best friend. And she still ain’t end the suspense yet. She searched the whole campus looking for me — and finally found me standing alone. “Why are you here?” she asked, followed by a rapid-fire round of a hundred questions. I didn’t answer a single one. not because I was deep… I just didn’t trust my voice not to crack.

Then she saw my cartoon mad face and, out of nowhere, just hugged me and said, “I love you.” I didn’t even wait a second — I blurted “I love you too.” Looking back, maybe I should’ve pretended to think for a moment — just to look cool. But hey, when your crush hugs you and says I love you, logic takes a coffee break. 😄
